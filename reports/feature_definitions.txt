
FEATURE DEFINITIONS FOR MULTI-OUTPUT RISK FORECASTING
======================================================

TARGET VARIABLES (What we predict):
1. Credit_Risk_Score
2. Liquidity_Ratio
3. Market_Volatility_Exposure

FIRM-LEVEL FEATURES (Company financial ratios):
1. Current_Ratio
2. Quick_Ratio
3. Leverage_Ratio
4. Profit_Margin

MACRO-ECONOMIC FEATURES (Economic indicators):
1. GDP Growth rate
2. CPI Inflation index
3. Policy Repo rate
4. Unemployment Rate(%)
5. Money Supply (M2)(000'Billion)

MARKET FEATURES (Market & volatility indicators):
1. Stock Market Index(Monthly_Close)
2. Stock Market Index(Monthly_Return)
3. Stock Market Index(Monthly_Volatility)
4. VIX(Monthly_Close)
5. VIX(Monthly_Return)
6. VIX(Monthly_Volatility)
7. 10-Year Government Bond Yield
8. AAA Corporate Bond Yield
9. Government Bond Yield (%) (10Y benchmark)
10. Corporate Bond Spread
11. Exchange Rate (USD/INR)(Monthly_Close)
12. Exchange Rate (USD/INR)(Monthly_Return)
13. Exchange Rate (USD/INR)(Monthly_Volatility)
